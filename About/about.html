About Us

Wildlife Bank is an An open-source initiative to keep the count on the population of endangered species left.
It is platform a bit inspired from Google Earth & World meter as combination of both. Where people
can gain education about species and also can know about their numbers too in real time. Also, people here can
report wildlife crime or death of the animal and people can read about. It will be like maintain an
information/reporting bank or library of animals. Also people here can report any wildlife animal they
spotted outside their zone through our WildlifeSpot feature so that we can inform concern authority. Apart from
these we have made analytics tools for the endangered species we are targetting.
